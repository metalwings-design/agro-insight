# Code of Conduct
## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level. We commit to acting in ways that contribute to a welcoming and inclusive environment.

## Standards
Positive behavior includes:

Using welcoming and inclusive language.

Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.

Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.

Focusing on what is best for the overall community.

## Unacceptable behavior includes:

Harassment, trolling, and personal or political attacks.

Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome attention.

Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.

Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing these standards. They have the right to remove or reject comments, commits, code, or issues that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Abusive or unacceptable behavior can be reported directly to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential.
